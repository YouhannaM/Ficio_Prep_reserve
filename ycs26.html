§ 02 · Vision

Where this goes.

Kitchens that operate just-in-time — where every ingredient is prepped seconds before it's needed, at Michelin-grade quality, with nanometer-precision consistency, and an optimization layer that matches idle capacity to live demand across a continent.
01 / Wedge
FicioPrep
Drop-in ingredient prep with multi-modal AI quality control. ~90% classification accuracy on potatoes alone.
02 / Expansion
FicioChef
Generalized cooking — searing, stirring, frying. The same AI brain, different end-effector.
03 / Platform
Kitchen OS
Idle kitchens become a 24/7 production network. Off-hours capacity rented to ghost brands and delivery platforms.
§ 03 · Timing

Why now.

01
VLA models crossed the threshold
Vision-language-action models can now generalize across food objects without per-SKU training. Two years ago this was a research demo. Today it runs on a Jetson at the edge.
02

75% turnover. $218B in food waste annually. Operators are no longer asking if they should automate — they're asking who can deploy first.
03
Hardware finally affordable
Collaborative arms at commodity prices. Custom end-effectors at $7.5K COGS. We deliver the same outcome as a $1M install for $1,500/month.
